Sporting Events I Attended

For a few years I made posts about baseball games I’d attended in the preceding year. I haven’t done that since the pandemic, but I’ve been tracking the sporting events I’ve attended in my own notes. Since my sporting interests have diversified, it doesn’t make as much sense to separate posts into seasons, the way it did when I only really followed baseball. This is meant to be an ongoing post where I’ll track events I’ve attended, including many different sports.

Score Starting Pitcher

2021-09-29

Score Starting Pitcher
New York Yankees 5 Gerrit Cole
Toronto Blue Jays 6 José Berríos

Berríos was perfect through 4.2 innings. Bo Bichette hit two home runs. Marcus Semien hit his 44th home run, which some considered to set a record for home runs in a single season by a second baseman, although he also hit home runs as a shortstop and possibly DH, if I remember correctly.

2021-10-03

Score Starting Pitcher
Baltimore Orioles 4 Bruce Zimmermann
Toronto Blue Jays 12 Hyun-Jin Ryu

Vladimir Guerrero Jr. hit his 48th home run, breaking Eddie Mathews’ record for home runs in a season at age 22 or younger. George Springer hit a grand slam.

In order to make the playoffs, Toronto needed either the Yankees or Red Sox to lose. The Yankees were tied 0-0 until the ninth inning of their game. The Red Sox were down 5-1 but came back to win while the game was broadcast on the jumbotron in Toronto.

Score Starting Quarterback
BC Lions 29 Michael Reilly

2021-12-05

Score Starting Quarterback
Hamilton Tiger-Cats 27 Jeremiah Masoli

After the game, Hamilton fans started throwing things at Argos players and some of the Argos climbed barriers towards them.
